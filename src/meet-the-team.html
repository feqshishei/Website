Corey Schimpf

Principal Investigator - University at Buffalo

Corey Schimpf is the Principal Investigator (PI) for the project. He oversees the curriculum and technology development and co-leads the research design with the team. He is an Assistant Professor of Engineering Education at the University at Buffalo. He has research interests in design thinking, human-centered design and developing scaffolding tools for supporting learning in complex domains.
