Welcome to the Mobile Design Studio Project!

This is a National Science Foundation Discovery Research K-12 (DRK12 ) grant lead by University at Buffalo with collaborators at University of Michigan and the Concord Consortium.

MODS focuses on front-end engineering design challenges that are integrated with Earth Science topics using an innovative NGSS-aligned curriculum, collaborative web-based software with an embedded AI design mentor to support students.

The project targets student outcomes including design thinking, socio-scientific thinking, engineering and science integration to prepare students for complex future problems they will face. This site is intended for researchers and teachers interested in K-12 engineering design, Earth and environmental science.
